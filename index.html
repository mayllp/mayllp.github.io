Meeting FAQ

How long has your company been in business?

We established our company in 2010 and have been specializing in outdoor equipment manufacturing and supply for over 13 years.

Do you offer product customization?

Yes, we offer customization services for most of our products. This includes color options, material variations, and functional modifications based on your requirements.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For most items, it ranges from 500 to 1000 units. Please contact our sales team for specific MOQ information on products you're interested in.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we can provide samples for most products. There may be a small fee for samples which can be credited toward your first order. Shipping costs for samples are typically the responsibility of the customer.

How do you ensure product quality?

We maintain strict quality control processes throughout production. This includes raw material inspection, in-process quality checks, and final product testing. We also offer a 1-year warranty on most products against manufacturing defects.

What are your shipping methods?

We ship via various methods including DHL, FedEx, UPS, and sea freight. The shipping method depends on the quantity ordered and your specific requirements. We'll provide shipping quotes based on your order volume and destination.
